V-USDT: Vision-Based UAV Swarm Detection and Tracking by Leveraging Swarm Formation Constraints

Venue: IEEE MDM 2025 Type: Conference Paper DOI: 10.1109/MDM65600.2025.00026 Swarm Tracking Formation Constraints
Back to Projects Paper Code Demo

Problem / Why It Matters

UAV swarm targets often look similar and are frequently occluded, making long-term identity association unstable.

Reliable temporal association improves downstream swarm-state understanding and coordinated autonomy behavior.

Method / Key Contributions

  • Formation-aware temporal association for identity consistency
  • Detection-tracking integration tuned for small airborne targets
  • Swarm geometry constraints for ambiguity reduction in overlap conditions

Results / Impact

MOTA (reported baseline -> method)

87.1 -> 90.4

IDF1 (reported baseline -> method)

91.1 -> 93.1

Misses (reported baseline -> method)

78.24 -> 63.29

Reported values indicate baseline-to-method improvement trends under the evaluation setup described in the paper.

Qualitative comparison shows improved trajectory continuity during overlap and weak-observation intervals.

My Role

I led task framing, method integration, experimental design, analysis, and manuscript writing for this work.
