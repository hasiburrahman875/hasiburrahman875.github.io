KRAfT: Kalman Residual Diffusion with Formation Awareness for UAV Swarm Tracking

Venue: IEEE ICPR 2026 Status: Accepted Domain: UAV Swarm Tracking Formation-Aware Missing Observation Recovery
Back to Projects Paper Code Demo

Problem

Swarm tracking performance can collapse when targets overlap, disappear temporarily, or produce ambiguous motion cues.

Why It Matters

Reliable identity continuity is essential for swarm behavior analysis and downstream autonomous decision logic.

Method

KRAfT combines Kalman residual refinement with formation-aware constraints and conservative recovery for missing observations.

Key Contributions

  • Formation-aware UAV swarm tracking
  • Kalman residual refinement for motion-consistent recovery
  • Conservative recovery strategy to reduce false associations during missing observations
