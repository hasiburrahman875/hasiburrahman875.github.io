KRAfT: Kalman Residual Diffusion with Formation Awareness for UAV Swarm Tracking

Status: Accepted Venue: IEEE ICPR 2026 Domain: UAV Swarm Tracking
Back to Projects Code Demo Paper Presentation

Problem Statement

UAV swarm tracking in real scenes is difficult when objects are small, move rapidly, overlap, or disappear temporarily. Standard tracking pipelines can drift during ambiguous motion and heavy clutter.

Research Gap

Existing approaches often underuse swarm formation structure and cannot reliably recover from missed targets without introducing aggressive false associations.

Methodology

KRAfT combines residual motion modeling, diffusion-style temporal refinement, and formation-aware constraints with conservative recovery to stabilize association under difficult swarm dynamics.

  • Temporal residual modeling to capture hard motion transitions
  • Formation-aware reasoning to preserve swarm geometry
  • Association and update rules designed for conservative recovery
  • Robust tracking behavior under missing and ambiguous observations
