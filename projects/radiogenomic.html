Radiogenomic Brain Tumor Classification from MRI Sequences

Biomedical AI Extension Status: Poster Recognition Pathways 2026 Symposium MRI Radiogenomics
Back to Projects Poster Request Materials

Problem / Why It Matters

Multimodal MRI analysis for radiogenomic brain tumor characterization is challenging due to heterogeneous image quality and subtle biomarker signatures.

Robust classification behavior matters for translational clinical decision support and cross-site reliability.

Method / Key Contributions

  • MRI-based radiogenomic classification workflow exploration
  • Reliability-oriented multimodal modeling direction
  • Cross-domain transfer from robust perception principles

Results / Impact

This work received Top 3 Best Poster recognition at the Pathways 2026 Symposium (NextGen Precision Health).

This project is presented as a translational extension direction and is not represented here as a peer-reviewed full paper.

My Role

I contributed to problem framing, multimodal modeling direction, experimental analysis, and poster communication.
