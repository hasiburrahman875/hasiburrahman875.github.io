Radiogenomic Brain Tumor Classification from MRI Sequences

Domain: Biomedical AI Recognition: Top 3 Best Poster, Pathways 2026 Symposium Context: NextGen Precision Health
Back to Projects Code Demo Paper Presentation

Problem Statement

Brain tumor characterization from MRI can benefit from radiogenomic modeling that links imaging phenotypes to clinically relevant molecular patterns.

Research Gap

Robust multimodal integration and clinically reliable decision support remain challenging when MRI data are heterogeneous and biomarkers are subtle.

Methodology

This translational direction explores multimodal MRI sequence modeling for radiogenomic classification, drawing on robust feature learning principles developed in dynamic visual perception research.

  • Multimodal MRI sequence handling for tumor characterization
  • Data-driven classification for radiogenomic interpretation
  • Cross-disciplinary integration between computer vision and biomedical AI

Results

This work received Top 3 Best Poster recognition at the Pathways 2026 Symposium hosted by NextGen Precision Health.

Qualitative Comparison

Relative to unimodal analysis workflows, multimodal radiogenomic modeling is intended to provide richer diagnostic context and better support translational decision pathways.
