Project Detail

An Augmented Dataset for Vision-based Unmanned Aerial Vehicles Detection and Tracking

Venue: IEEE AIPR 2023 DOI: 10.1109/AIPR60534.2023.10440657 Type: Dataset + Benchmark
Back to Projects Code/Dataset Demo Video Paper Presentation

Problem

UAV detection and tracking research needs realistic datasets that include weak targets, clutter, camera motion, and dynamic scene conditions.

Why It Matters

Strong benchmarking resources are essential for fair comparison and practical robustness analysis in airborne perception research.

Method

This project curates and augments UAV sequence data to support reproducible evaluation for detection and tracking under realistic stress conditions.

  • Augmented sequence construction for detection and tracking.
  • Benchmark support for challenging real-world variation.
  • Repository release for reusable experimental workflows.

Results / Impact

Published at IEEE AIPR 2023, this dataset contributes a practical evaluation base for weak-target and motion-heavy UAV scenarios.

Qualitative Comparison

Compared with smaller and less varied resources, this augmented benchmark introduces stronger variation for robustness-oriented evaluation.
