Teaching Philosophy

How I Teach

  • Start from intuition, then formalize with models and algorithms.
  • Use real datasets and practical systems to ground abstract concepts.
  • Encourage structured experimentation, not trial-and-error coding.
  • Emphasize technical communication: write, present, and defend ideas clearly.

What I Mentor For

  • Independent problem formulation and hypothesis design.
  • Responsible model evaluation and failure analysis.
  • Team collaboration and reproducible research habits.
  • Confidence in converting coursework into publishable-quality work.
