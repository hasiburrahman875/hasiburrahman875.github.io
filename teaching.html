Teaching

Secondary profile page on teaching experience, mentoring style, and instructional approach.

Teaching Experience

  • Graduate Teaching Assistant, Missouri S&T (during PhD)
  • Lecturer, CSE, BUBT (2018-2022)
  • Course and lab support across data-intensive computing and applied CS topics

Courses and Technical Areas

  • Data Mining, Internet of Things, Operating Systems, Data Structures
  • Big data and systems tools: Hadoop, Spark, Hive, HBase, MongoDB, MATLAB
  • Research-aligned mentoring in machine learning and computer vision projects

Mentoring Style

  • Guide problem formulation and experimental planning first
  • Emphasize reproducibility, ablation clarity, and failure analysis
  • Strengthen communication skills for papers, posters, and technical presentations

Teaching Philosophy

I teach by linking intuition to formalism, then converting theory into implementation and evaluation practice. The goal is to help students become confident independent problem-solvers who can build and defend reliable technical work.
