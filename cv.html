CV Overview

Md Hasibur Rahman is a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Missouri University of Science & Technology. His work focuses on robust multimodal perception for sparse, noisy, and safety-critical sensing environments, with research spanning UAV tracking, heterogeneous sensor fusion, sparse 3D perception, and uncertainty-aware modeling.

Research Interests

  • Robust multimodal perception for safety-critical environments
  • UAV-based tiny object detection and tracking
  • LiDAR/radar/vision fusion under sparse and noisy sensing
  • Uncertainty-aware tracking and filtering
  • Sparse 3D perception and deployable real-world AI systems
  • Translational biomedical AI and radiogenomic imaging
