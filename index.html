Research Summary

My research develops robust multimodal perception systems for sparse, noisy, and safety-critical environments. I focus on UAV-based detection and tracking, tiny airborne object localization, heterogeneous multi-sensor fusion, sparse 3D perception, and uncertainty-aware tracking.
