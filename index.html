Research Areas

UAV Perception & Small-Object Detection

Core dissertation area: tiny airborne object detection from moving drones under clutter, motion, camouflage, and occlusion.

UAV Swarm Tracking & Temporal Association

Formation-aware multi-object tracking, missing-observation recovery, and motion-consistent trajectory reasoning.

Multimodal Sensing & Reliable Fusion

Camera/LiDAR/radar-style sensing, uncertainty-aware inference, and robust evaluation under degraded observations.

GeoAI & Biomedical AI Extensions

Reliability and generalization for SAR-optical-weather GeoAI and multimodal MRI/radiogenomic biomedical AI.
