Research Summary

I develop reliable learning systems for tracking, dynamic behavior analysis, and robust inference from time-varying image sequences. My work spans small-target detection, temporal association, and multimodal sensing under clutter, motion, partial observability, and noisy measurements.

Current focus: robust UAV swarm tracking, spatiotemporal attention for tiny airborne objects, deployment-oriented 2D/3D sensing pipelines, and uncertainty-aware model behavior in real-world settings.
