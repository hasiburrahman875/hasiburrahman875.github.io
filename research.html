Research

My research centers on robust computer vision and multimodal AI for dynamic, uncertain environments. My dissertation work focuses on UAV perception, tiny airborne object detection, and UAV swarm tracking. I also extend these reliability-driven methods to GeoAI and biomedical AI.

Core Research Theme: Robust UAV Perception

  • Small airborne object detection under moving-camera conditions
  • Temporal context integration for weak/faint target recovery
  • Robustness under clutter, camouflage, and partial occlusion
  • Deployment-oriented evaluation beyond clean benchmark conditions

Core Research Theme: UAV Swarm Tracking

  • Temporal association for visually similar targets in dense swarm scenes
  • Formation constraints for geometry-consistent tracking
  • Recovery under missing observations and intermittent detections
  • Trajectory continuity and identity stability under ambiguity

Methodological Theme: Reliable Multimodal AI

  • Uncertainty-aware multimodal fusion and reliability analysis
  • Performance under sensor degradation and missing observations
  • OOD and stress-testing strategies for robust deployment claims
  • Calibration and trustworthy model behavior assessment

Extension Theme: Domain-Generalized GeoAI

AgriShift investigates selective domain residualization and reliability-aware SAR-optical-weather fusion for climate-smart agriculture.

  • Sentinel-1 SAR + Sentinel-2 optical + weather/environmental covariates
  • Generalization across unseen regions, years, and climate regimes
  • Robustness under cloud corruption and modality gaps

Extension Theme: Biomedical AI

  • MRI-based radiogenomic classification as a translational direction
  • Robust multimodal classification under heterogeneous clinical data
  • Reliability-oriented transfer of perception principles to healthcare

Current 2026 Research Agenda

  • Robust UAV tracking under missing observations and occlusions
  • Multimodal sensor fusion under uncertainty and sensor shift
  • Domain-generalized GeoAI for climate-smart agriculture
  • Reliable biomedical imaging and radiogenomic modeling
  • VLM/foundation-model-assisted weak-target detection
